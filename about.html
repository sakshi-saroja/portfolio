About Me


Sakshi Venkatraman is a junior at NYU majoring in Politics with minors in Spanish and Web Programming and Applications. She's a multimedia journalist with experience in writing, photography, and audio for various publications, broadcast networks, and radio stations in New York City and Texas. She also has experience working with Python and programming basic websites (in fact, she programmed this one). Sakshi served as editor-in-chief of NYU's Washington Square News last year, and she is currently an intern with NBC News Digital, where she covers national news like the coronavirus pandemic.
